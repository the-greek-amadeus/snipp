About Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

A chronic condition including attention difficulties, hyperactivity and impulsiveness.

ADHD often begins in childhood and can persist into adulthood. It may contribute to low self-esteem, troubled relationships and difficulty at school or work.

Symptoms

Symptoms include limited attention and hyperactivity.

People may experience:

  • Behavioural: aggression, excitability, fidgeting, hyperactivity, impulsivity, irritability, lack of restraint, or persistent repetition of words or actions
  • Cognitive: absent-mindedness, difficulty focusing, forgetfulness, problems paying attention, or short attention span
  • Mood: anger, anxiety, boredom, excitement, or mood swings
